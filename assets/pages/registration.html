  • entry requirements

    Possession of five (5) credits at not more than two sittings in O’level results (WAEC or NECO); these must include English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and Biology. A combination of WAEC and NECO is acceptable. NABTEB is also acceptable. Any other qualification in Nursing will be an added advantage.

  • how to apply

    How to Apply for FNPH Kaduna Post Basic Nursing Admission Form
    Application forms can be obtained from the School of Post-Basic Psychiatric Nursing, Federal Neuro-Psychiatric Hospital, Barnawa, Kaduna.
    APPLICATION FEE: A non-refundable fee of N10,000 only for the Application form payable through Remita in favour of Federal Neuro-Psychiatric Hospital, Kaduna
    SUBMISSION OF FORMS: Completed forms should be submitted with photocopies of relevant credentials and two passport photographs (coloured on white background) to the institution on or before Friday, 17th March 2023.
    PRE-TEST DATE: Tuesday 21st March 2023 (9 am prompt)
    INTERVIEW DATE: Wednesday 22nd March 2023 (9 am Prompt)
    NOTE: Please note that successful candidates would be expected to resume training on 24th April 2023
